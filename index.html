Key Operational Departments

The Super Executive team oversees several crucial departments (Operations, Human Resources, Public Relations, Server Moderation, and Support), ensuring specialized attention is given to every aspect of the Drip Cafe experience:

Operations Department (OD)

Manages the day-to-day running of the cafe and server. Focuses on efficiency, resource management, and ensuring all standard procedures are followed across the LR, MR, and HR teams.

Human Resources (HR)

Responsible for staffing, training, and policy enforcement. Handles promotions, disciplinary actions, and ensuring a positive, professional working environment. A permanent, crucial department.

Public Relations (PR)

Focuses on community engagement, partnerships, and brand image. Manages official communications, events, and ensures the Drip Cafe maintains its welcoming and professional reputation. A permanent, crucial department.

Server Moderation (SM)

Manages in-game conduct and rule enforcement to ensure a safe and secure environment. Focuses on handling exploiters, rule-breakers, and providing quick resolutions to disruptive behavior. A permanent, crucial team.

Support Team (ST)

Handles external and internal communications, ticketing and general inquiries via Discord. Provides detailed, timely support to the community and staff on policies and issues.

Official Rules and Policies

Adherence to these rules is mandatory for all members, staff, and guests within Drip Cafe's game and community platforms. Violations will result in warnings, kicks, bans, or rank removal, determined by the severity of the offense.

General Community Rules (All Users)

  • Respect and Conduct: Always maintain a positive, welcoming, and respectful attitude towards all users and staff. Bullying, harassment, or derogatory language is strictly forbidden.
  • ROBLOX ToS: All users must adhere strictly to the Roblox Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • No Exploiting or Hacking: Using any third-party software, exploits, or hacks to gain an unfair advantage or disrupt the game is an automatic, permanent ban.
  • Spam and Trolling: Do not spam chat, advertise other groups, or purposefully troll or bait staff/other users.
  • Listening to Staff: High Ranks (HRs) and above have authority; please follow their instructions promptly.

Staff Conduct and Expectations (LR to HR)

  • Professionalism: Always use proper grammar when serving customers (LR/MR) and when communicating with fellow staff. Maintain a professional demeanor.
  • No Begging: Do not beg, spam, or constantly ask for promotions, ranks, or admin powers from any HR or Executive. Promotions are earned through merit.
  • Activity Requirement: All staff must meet the minimum activity requirements set by the Human Resources (HR) Department to maintain their rank. Consistent inactivity will lead to demotion or removal.
  • Admin Abuse: Staff with administrative tools are forbidden from using them for personal gain, targeting individuals, or trolling. Misuse of tools results in immediate termination.
  • Honesty: Always be honest in your reports and communication. Dishonesty undermines team trust and is a serious breach of policy.
